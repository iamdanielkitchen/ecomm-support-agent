# How to start a return

Starting a return takes about five minutes. You'll need your order number and email address, then we'll send you a prepaid return label and instructions. Once your item arrives at our warehouse, your refund processes within 5–7 business days.

## What you need before you start

Have your order number handy. It starts with FG- followed by six digits (for example, FG-123456) and appears in your order confirmation email. You'll also need the email address you used at checkout.

If your item is still within the 30-day return window and isn't marked final sale, you're all set. See our guide on return eligibility and the 30-day window for details on what qualifies.

## Request your return authorization

Visit the returns page and enter your order number and email address. Select the item you want to return and choose a reason from the dropdown menu. This helps us improve, but it does not affect your eligibility.

You'll receive a return authorization email within a few minutes. That email contains your RMA number and a prepaid shipping label as a PDF attachment. Print the label or display it on your phone at the carrier location.

## Pack and ship your item

Place the item in its original packaging if you still have it. If not, use a sturdy box that protects the item during transit. Attach the prepaid label to the outside of the package, covering or removing any old shipping labels.

Drop off your package at any location that accepts the carrier shown on your label. You do not need to schedule a pickup. Keep your drop-off receipt until you see the refund land in your account.

Unopened items ship back for free. If you opened the item but it remains in original condition, a $7.95 return shipping fee applies and will be deducted from your refund. For more on how fees work, see return shipping fees.

## What happens after the warehouse receives your return

Our team inspects the item within two business days of arrival. If everything checks out, your refund processes to the original payment method. Most refunds appear within 5–7 business days, though your bank may take an additional day or two to post the credit.

You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund processes. If there's an issue with the return—damage during shipping or missing components—we'll contact you at the email address on file.

## FAQ

**What if I lost my order number?** Check your email inbox for "Fieldstone Goods order confirmation" or contact Ember, our support agent, through the chat widget. She can look up your order using your email and shipping address.

**Can I return part of a multi-item order?** Yes. During the return request, select only the items you want to send back. The rest of your order is unaffected.

**Do I need to include the original receipt?** No. Your RMA number ties the return to your order, so you do not need to include a packing slip or printed receipt.
