# Return eligibility and the 30-day window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to return most items. The clock starts when the package arrives at your address, not when you place the order. Unopened items return for free; opened items in original condition have a $7.95 return shipping fee. Three categories are never eligible: final-sale items, items outside the 30-day window, and items that are worn or damaged beyond their original condition.

## When the 30-day window starts and ends

The return window begins on the delivery date shown in your shipping confirmation email. If your order arrived on March 15, you have until April 14 to initiate the return. Orders placed earlier but delivered later still follow the delivery date, so a week in transit does not shorten your window.

You must start the return process by the end of day 30. We use the timestamp when you submit the return request, not when the package arrives back at our warehouse. See our guide on How to start a return for step-by-step instructions.

## What counts as unopened versus opened

Unopened means the product is still in its original packaging with all seals, tags, and protective wrapping intact. For items like the Cast Iron Skillet (FG-KIT-001) or the Ceramic Mixing Bowl Set (FG-KIT-003), this means the cardboard box is sealed and the product has not been removed.

Opened items are those you have removed from packaging, inspected, or tested. You can return opened items as long as they remain in original condition: no scratches, stains, chips, or missing pieces. The Walnut Cutting Board (FG-KIT-002) can be returned if you unboxed it, but not if it shows knife marks or water damage. Opened returns incur a $7.95 return shipping fee to cover our processing costs. Unopened returns ship free.

## Three categories that are never eligible

**Final-sale items** cannot be returned under any circumstance. These are marked as final sale at checkout and in your order confirmation. Examples include the Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001) and Seasonal Tea Towels (FG-SAL-002). For the full list and reasoning, see our guide on Final sale items.

**Items outside the 30-day window** cannot be processed as standard returns. If you discover an issue after day 30, contact us. We cannot promise an exception, but a specialist will review your situation.

**Items worn, used, or damaged** beyond their original condition are not eligible. This includes cookware with food residue, linens with stains or pet hair, and rugs with stretched backing. If your item arrived damaged or defective, that is a separate process—see our guide on Damaged or defective items for next steps.

## FAQ

**Does the 30-day window include weekends and holidays?**  
Yes. The window is 30 calendar days, not business days. If day 30 falls on a weekend, you still need to submit your return request by that date.

**What if I received my order as a gift?**  
The same 30-day window applies, starting from the delivery date to the recipient's address. The recipient can initiate the return using the order number from the packing slip. Refunds return to the original purchaser's payment method.

**Can I return one item from a multi-item order?**  
Yes. Each item in your order is eligible independently, as long as it meets the conditions above. You can keep the Linen Throw Blanket (FG-HOM-001) and return the Espresso Cups (FG-KIT-004) from the same order.
