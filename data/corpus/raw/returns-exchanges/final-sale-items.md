# Final sale items

Final-sale items cannot be returned or exchanged once your order ships. These are typically seasonal or limited-run products offered at a discount, and the final-sale designation appears clearly on the product page before you add the item to your cart. If a final-sale item arrives damaged or defective, we will replace it or issue a refund.

## Which items are final sale

Final-sale products are marked with a "Final Sale" badge on the product page, directly below the price. Currently, our final-sale catalog includes:

- **FG-SAL-001** — Holiday Candle — $18.00
- **FG-SAL-002** — Seasonal Tea Towels, Set of 2 — $22.00

The final-sale list changes with the season. Before completing your purchase, double-check the product page and your cart summary to confirm which items carry the final-sale restriction.

## Why some items are final sale

We use final-sale pricing for seasonal goods, limited editions, and clearance inventory. These products move quickly, and the discounted price reflects the no-return policy. Final-sale items help us offer you great value on curated pieces while managing inventory turns responsibly.

## What to do if a final-sale item arrives damaged

Final sale means no returns for change of mind, but it does not mean you are stuck with a damaged product. If your final-sale item arrives broken, defective, or visibly damaged in transit, contact Ember with your order number and a photo of the issue. We will send a replacement if stock permits, or issue a full refund if the item is no longer available. See our guide on damaged or defective items for details on how to document and report the problem.

Standard return eligibility and the 30-day window do not apply to final-sale items, but our quality guarantee does. You should receive what you ordered in good condition, regardless of the sale terms.

## FAQ

**Can I exchange a final-sale item for a different size or color?**  
No. Final-sale items cannot be returned or exchanged for any reason except damage or defect.

**Will I know before I buy that something is final sale?**  
Yes. The product page displays a "Final Sale" badge below the price, and your cart summary repeats the designation before checkout.

**What if I order a final-sale item by mistake?**  
Once the order ships, the final-sale policy takes effect. If you realize the mistake before shipment, contact Ember immediately—we may be able to cancel the order, though we cannot guarantee it.
