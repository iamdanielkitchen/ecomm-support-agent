# Return shipping fees

Returns are free for unopened items in original packaging. If you've opened an item but it's still in its original condition, we charge a $7.95 return shipping fee per order. This fee covers the cost of return logistics and helps us keep prices fair for all customers.

## When returns are free

You will not pay a return shipping fee if every item in your return is unopened and in its original packaging. The item must be unused, with all tags and protective materials intact. We provide a prepaid return label at no charge.

This applies to standard-return items only. Final-sale items like the Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001) and Seasonal Tea Towels (FG-SAL-002) cannot be returned regardless of condition.

## The $7.95 opened-item fee

If you've opened an item but kept it in original condition—no damage, stains, or missing pieces—you can still return it within 30 days of delivery. We'll deduct $7.95 from your refund to cover return shipping costs.

The fee applies once per return order, not per item. If you're returning three opened products in a single return, you pay $7.95 total. See our guide on return eligibility and the 30-day window for details on condition requirements.

## Mixed returns: unopened and opened items

If your return includes both unopened and opened items, the $7.95 fee applies to the entire return. We assess the fee based on whether any item in the return has been opened.

For example, if you're returning an unopened Walnut Cutting Board (FG-KIT-002) and an opened Cast Iron Skillet (FG-KIT-001), the $7.95 fee will be deducted from your total refund. To avoid the fee, you could submit two separate returns—one for the unopened board, one for the opened skillet—but most customers find it simpler to send everything together.

## How the fee is processed

When you start a return through our system, you'll see the fee displayed before you confirm. We deduct it from your refund after the warehouse receives and inspects your items. Refunds land on your original payment method within 5–7 business days of warehouse receipt.

If you have questions about whether your item qualifies as unopened, check the packaging when it arrives. Original shrink wrap, sealed boxes, and attached hang tags all indicate unopened status. For step-by-step instructions, see how to start a return.

## FAQ

**Can I avoid the fee by using my own shipping label?**  
No. The $7.95 fee applies to opened items regardless of how you ship them back. We provide a prepaid label for convenience, but using your own carrier will not waive the fee.

**Does the fee apply to defective or damaged items?**  
No. If an item arrives defective or damaged, return shipping is free and we will issue a full refund with no deduction. Contact Ember with your order number (format FG-NNNNNN) and photos of the issue to begin that process.

**What if I'm returning only final-sale items?**  
Final-sale items cannot be returned, so no return shipping fee applies. You cannot initiate a return for items marked final sale at the time of purchase.
