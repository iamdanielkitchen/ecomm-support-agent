# Exchanges vs. returns

Fieldstone processes all exchanges as a return followed by a new order. We do not hold or swap items in place. This means you'll receive a refund for your original purchase, then place a separate order for the replacement item. While this takes a few more days than a direct swap, it gives you full control over what you order next and ensures accurate inventory.

## Why we process exchanges as two separate transactions

An exchange at Fieldstone works in two steps. First, you return the item you no longer want using our standard return process. Once our warehouse receives and inspects it, we issue a refund to your original payment method. Second, you place a new order for the item you'd prefer—different size, color, or product entirely.

We handle exchanges this way because our inventory updates in real time. Holding an item during a return creates timing issues: the replacement might sell out, or your return might not meet our condition guidelines. Processing each step separately ensures you see current stock, current pricing, and clear refund timelines. See our guide on refund timelines for details on when your refund will land.

## How to exchange an item

Start your return the same way you would for any unwanted item. Visit the returns page, enter your email and order number (format FG-NNNNNN), and select the item to return. Print the prepaid label and ship the package within five days.

Opened items in original condition carry a $7.95 return shipping fee, deducted from your refund. Unopened items return free. Final-sale items marked in the catalog—currently FG-SAL-001 and FG-SAL-002—cannot be returned or exchanged.

Once your return reaches our warehouse, expect your refund within 5–7 business days. After the refund posts, place a new order for the replacement. Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days; expedited shipping takes 1–2 business days.

## What this means for timing and inventory

Because an exchange is two transactions, the full process takes longer than an in-place swap. Budget roughly two weeks from the day you ship your return to the day your replacement arrives, assuming standard shipping both ways.

If the item you want is low in stock, place your new order as soon as you ship the return—you do not need to wait for the refund to post before ordering. Just know that you'll pay for the new order upfront. The refund will arrive on your original payment method a few days later.

Items can sell out between your return and your new order. Check the product page before you return to confirm the replacement is in stock. We cannot reserve inventory during a return window.

## FAQ

**Can I exchange a final-sale item?**  
No. Items marked final sale in the catalog cannot be returned or exchanged. This applies to FG-SAL-001 (Holiday Candle) and FG-SAL-002 (Seasonal Tea Towels, Set of 2).

**Will I pay shipping twice?**  
You'll pay return shipping if your item was opened ($7.95, deducted from your refund). Your new order will have its own shipping charge—standard or expedited, depending on what you choose at checkout. Unopened returns ship free.

**What if the new item costs more or less than the original?**  
You'll receive a full refund for the returned item (minus any return shipping fee), then pay the current price for the new order. If prices have changed between orders, you'll see the difference reflected in the two transactions.
