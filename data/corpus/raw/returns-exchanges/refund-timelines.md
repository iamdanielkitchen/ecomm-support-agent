# Refund timelines

Your refund will land on your original payment method within 5–7 business days after our warehouse receives and processes your return. This window accounts for bank processing time and varies by card issuer. The clock starts when the warehouse confirms receipt, not when you print the return label or drop off the package.

## When the refund timer begins

The 5–7 business day window begins the moment our warehouse scans your returned item into inventory and confirms the condition matches our return eligibility standards. Shipping time does not count toward this period. If you print a return label on Monday and the carrier delivers the package to our warehouse on Friday, the refund timer starts Friday.

You will receive an email confirming warehouse receipt. That confirmation marks the beginning of the 5–7 business day refund window. If you started your return through our returns portal and provided your order number (format FG-NNNNNN) and email address, the confirmation goes to that address automatically.

## Why 5–7 business days instead of instant

Once our warehouse processes your return, we issue the refund immediately. The 5–7 business day range reflects how long banks and card networks take to post credits. Credit cards typically post refunds within five business days. Debit cards sometimes take the full seven. Weekends and bank holidays extend the timeline, since those are not business days.

We cannot expedite this period. The delay occurs between our payment processor and your bank. If seven business days have passed since the warehouse receipt email and the refund has not posted, contact your card issuer first. They can confirm whether the credit is pending on their end.

## Checking refund status

Your warehouse receipt email includes the refund amount and the date we released the funds. If you need to verify whether the warehouse received your return, check the tracking number on your return label. Delivered status means the package arrived; warehouse receipt occurs within one business day of delivery in most cases.

For returns with a $7.95 return shipping fee (opened items in original condition), the refunded amount will be your original order total minus that fee. Final-sale items cannot be returned, so no refund is possible for those SKUs. See our guide on return eligibility and the 30-day window for details on which items qualify.

## What happens if the timeline exceeds seven days

If eight or more business days have passed since your warehouse receipt email, and your bank confirms no pending credit, reach out through our support chat. The agent will verify the refund was issued and, if needed, escalate to a billing specialist. Have your order number and the warehouse receipt email ready.

Payment method changes also affect timing. If the original card is closed or expired, the issuing bank may return the funds to us. In that case, a specialist will coordinate an alternative refund method. This adds time but ensures you receive the full amount.

## FAQ

**Can I get a refund before you receive the return?**  
No. The warehouse must confirm receipt and inspect the item before issuing a refund. This protects against cases where a package is lost in transit or arrives in a condition that does not meet return guidelines.

**Will the refund show as pending before it posts?**  
Sometimes. Some card issuers display pending credits; others do not show the transaction until it fully posts. Check with your bank if you want visibility into pending activity.

**Does the 5–7 day window apply to all payment methods?**  
Yes. Credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets tied to a card all follow the same 5–7 business day standard once we release the refund.
