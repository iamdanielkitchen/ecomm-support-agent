# Order confirmation emails

You receive an order confirmation email within minutes of placing your order. It includes your order number, items purchased, total amount, and estimated delivery date. If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or use your email address to retrieve the order details.

## What's in your confirmation email

The confirmation email arrives from orders@fieldstonegoods.com with the subject line "Your Fieldstone order is confirmed." It contains:

- Your order number (format FG-NNNNNN)
- A list of items with SKUs and individual prices
- Shipping address and method
- Order total and payment method (last four digits)
- Estimated delivery date

The order number is your primary reference for tracking, returns, and support requests. Store this email or note the order number somewhere accessible.

## If you don't receive the email

Check your spam or promotions folder first. Email filters sometimes catch order confirmations. Search for "fieldstonegoods.com" or "order confirmed" to locate it.

If the email still doesn't appear after 30 minutes, the order likely did not complete. Common reasons include payment authorization failures or session timeouts. Try placing your order again. If the second attempt also produces no confirmation, contact Ember for help.

You cannot resend a confirmation email to a different address after placing an order. The confirmation always goes to the email address entered at checkout.

## Retrieving a lost order number

If you deleted the confirmation email or cannot locate it, you can retrieve your order details using your email address and basic order information. See our guide on finding your order number for step-by-step instructions.

You'll need your email address and an approximate order date or item name. The system matches these details to orders in our database and displays your order number and current status. For tracking updates once you have the order number, see Tracking your order.

## FAQ

**Can I change the email address after placing an order?**  
No. The confirmation email and all order updates go to the address entered at checkout. If you need to update contact information for a pending order, Ember can help.

**What if I receive multiple confirmation emails for one order?**  
You should receive exactly one confirmation. Multiple emails may indicate duplicate orders. Check each order number to verify. If you see charges for orders you did not intend to place, contact support immediately.

**Do I get another email when the order ships?**  
Yes. You'll receive a shipping notification with tracking information once your order leaves the warehouse, typically within 1–2 business days of the confirmation email.
