# Shipping methods and delivery timelines

Fieldstone Goods offers two shipping options: standard delivery (3–5 business days) and expedited delivery (1–2 business days). Both options ship from our warehouse in the continental United States and include tracking from the moment your order leaves our facility. We do not offer same-day delivery.

## Standard shipping

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days after your order ships. This method works well for most orders and covers the full continental United States. Once your order leaves the warehouse, you will receive a tracking number by email so you can follow its progress. Business days exclude weekends and major holidays.

Orders placed before 2:00 PM Eastern on a business day typically ship the same day. Orders placed after that cutoff ship the next business day. See our guide on when will my order ship for more on processing timelines.

## Expedited shipping

Expedited shipping delivers in 1–2 business days after your order ships. Choose this option at checkout if you need something faster. The same 2:00 PM Eastern cutoff applies: orders placed before that time on a business day usually ship the same day, and orders placed after ship the next business day.

Expedited shipping costs more than standard, and the exact rate depends on your delivery address. You will see the cost before you confirm your order.

## Why we do not offer same-day delivery

Same-day delivery requires local inventory hubs and fulfillment infrastructure that we do not currently operate. Our fulfillment model centers on a single warehouse that ships nationwide, which lets us maintain quality control and competitive pricing. Expedited shipping remains the fastest option we can reliably offer across all regions.

## Tracking and international orders

All domestic orders include tracking. You can monitor your shipment through the link in your shipping confirmation email or by visiting the tracking your order page. For addresses outside the continental United States, delivery timelines and costs vary. Review our international shipping article for details on customs, duties, and expected transit times.

## FAQ

**Can I upgrade my order to expedited shipping after I place it?**  
You cannot change the shipping method once an order is confirmed. If your order has not yet shipped, contact us and we can cancel the original order so you can reorder with expedited shipping.

**What happens if my order does not arrive within the estimated window?**  
Delays occasionally occur due to carrier issues or weather. Check your tracking information first. If the tracking shows no movement for several days or the package appears lost, reach out and we will work with the carrier to resolve it.

**Do business days include Saturday?**  
No. Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. A standard order placed Thursday evening, for example, typically ships Friday and arrives the following Wednesday or Thursday.
