# Shipping to PO boxes, APO, and FPO addresses

We ship to most PO boxes and military addresses, but some items cannot reach these locations due to carrier restrictions. Standard shipping works for all eligible orders, while expedited shipping is unavailable for PO box and military destinations.

## What we ship to PO boxes

We can send orders to domestic PO boxes when the package fits the box dimensions and contains only items that ship via USPS. If your order includes only smaller items like the Ceramic Mixing Bowl Set (FG-KIT-003), Espresso Cups (FG-KIT-004), or Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001), standard shipping to a PO box works fine.

Larger items ship via freight or specialty carriers that cannot deliver to PO boxes. The Wool Area Rug, 5x7 (FG-HOM-002) always ships freight and cannot be delivered to a PO box address. If your cart includes a rug or other oversized item, you'll need to provide a physical street address at checkout.

Only standard shipping (3–5 business days) is available for PO box addresses. Expedited shipping requires a street address.

## Shipping to APO and FPO addresses

We ship to military addresses (APO, FPO, and DPO) using USPS. Standard shipping (3–5 business days domestic, 7–14 business days international) is the only method available for these destinations. Expedited shipping is not offered.

The same size restrictions apply: if an item ships via freight carrier, it cannot be delivered to an APO or FPO address. The Wool Area Rug, 5x7 (FG-HOM-002) cannot ship to military addresses. All other current catalog items are eligible.

Military addresses follow the same return policy as domestic orders. You have 30 days from delivery to return eligible items, though return shipping times may be longer. See our guide on shipping methods and delivery timelines for more about transit expectations.

## Address format at checkout

Enter your PO box or military address exactly as it appears in USPS records. For PO boxes, use the format "PO Box [number]" in the street address field. For military addresses, use the full APO, FPO, or DPO designation with the correct AA/AE/AP state code and ZIP code.

If the address format isn't recognized at checkout or if your order includes an oversized item, you'll see an error message before payment. Double-check the address formatting or remove any freight items from your cart.

## FAQ

**Can I use expedited shipping to a PO box?** No. Expedited shipping (1–2 business days) requires a physical street address. Only standard shipping is available for PO box and military addresses.

**Why can't the rug ship to my APO address?** The Wool Area Rug, 5x7 ships via freight carrier due to size and weight. Freight carriers cannot deliver to PO boxes or military addresses. Other items in our catalog ship USPS and work with APO/FPO destinations.

**Will my APO order take longer than domestic orders?** Military addresses overseas typically see 7–14 business day delivery windows instead of the standard 3–5 days. Domestic APO addresses follow the same timeline as other U.S. deliveries.
