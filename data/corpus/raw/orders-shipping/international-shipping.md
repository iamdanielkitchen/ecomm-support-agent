# International shipping

Fieldstone Goods currently ships to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. We handle the shipping logistics, but you are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees your country requires. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days, depending on your location and customs processing times.

## Where we ship

We ship to these countries:

- United States (all 50 states)
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- Australia

We do not ship to PO boxes or military APO/FPO addresses outside the US. If your shipping address falls outside these four countries, you cannot complete checkout at this time.

## Customs duties and taxes

You pay any customs duties, import taxes, or brokerage fees that your country assesses when the package arrives. These charges are separate from your Fieldstone order total and are collected by the courier or customs agency at delivery.

We cannot estimate these fees in advance because they vary by country, product category, and declared value. Your order confirmation email shows only the merchandise cost and our shipping charge. Check with your local customs authority if you need an estimate before ordering.

The package will include a commercial invoice showing the item value. Do not ask us to mark the shipment as a gift or declare a lower value — we cannot alter customs paperwork.

## Shipping timelines

International delivery takes 7–14 business days after your order ships. See our guide on shipping methods and delivery timelines for more about when orders leave our warehouse.

Transit time depends on:

- Your country and region
- Customs clearance speed
- Local courier delivery schedules

You'll receive a tracking number by email when your order ships. Tracking updates may pause for a few days while the package clears customs. This is normal.

## Returns from outside the US

The 30-day return window applies to international orders. You pay return shipping costs to send items back to our US warehouse.

If the item is unopened and in original condition, you receive a full refund of the merchandise cost once we process the return. Our original shipping charge and your return shipping cost are not refundable. Opened items that meet our return criteria have the standard $7.95 return processing fee deducted from the refund. See return shipping fees for details on how that fee applies.

Use a trackable shipping method when returning items internationally. We cannot issue a refund until the package arrives at our warehouse. Customs fees you paid when receiving your order are not refundable — contact your country's customs authority if you have questions about recovering those charges.

Final-sale items cannot be returned, even on international orders.

## FAQ

**Can I use expedited shipping for international orders?**  
No. Only standard international shipping is available. Delivery takes 7–14 business days regardless of the shipping option you select at checkout.

**What if my package is held in customs?**  
Contact your local courier or customs office with your tracking number. We cannot intervene in customs processes or expedite clearance. If the package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will refund your merchandise cost minus our original shipping charge.

**Do you ship to my country if it's not listed?**  
Not at this time. We ship only to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia. We cannot make exceptions or arrange special shipping to other destinations.
