# When will my order ship

Most orders ship within one business day of placement. Orders placed after 2 PM Eastern or on weekends ship the next business day. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking details once your order leaves our warehouse.

## Order processing timeline

We process orders Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern on a business day typically ship the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM ship the following business day.

Weekend orders ship on Monday. If you order Friday evening, your package will not ship until Monday at the earliest. The same applies to orders placed on federal holidays — they ship on the next business day.

Processing time is separate from delivery time. Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days after your order ships, while expedited shipping takes 1–2 business days. For complete details on delivery windows, see our guide on shipping methods and delivery timelines.

## Reading your order status

You can check your order status anytime by entering your email and order number on our order lookup page. Here's what each status means:

**Processing** — We've received your order and are preparing it for shipment. Most orders stay in this status for a few hours.

**Shipped** — Your order has left our warehouse and is with the carrier. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number. Use that number to follow your package in real time. Our article on tracking your order walks you through the process.

**Delivered** — The carrier has delivered your package to the address on file.

## What delays shipping

A few situations can extend processing time beyond one business day. High-demand periods like holidays may add an extra day. Incorrect or incomplete shipping addresses require manual verification, which delays handoff to the carrier.

Payment issues also pause shipment. If your payment method is declined, we'll email you at the address on file. Once you update your payment information, your order re-enters the processing queue.

## FAQ

**Can I change my shipping speed after I place my order?**  
If your order status is still "Processing," contact us right away. We can sometimes adjust shipping speed before the package leaves the warehouse, though we cannot guarantee it. Once the status changes to "Shipped," the carrier speed is locked.

**Will I get an email when my order ships?**  
Yes. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number and an estimated delivery date. If you do not see this email within two business days of ordering, check your spam folder or look up your order status directly. For more on what to expect, review our article on order confirmation emails.

**Do you ship on Saturdays?**  
No. Our warehouse operates Monday through Friday. Orders ship on business days only.
