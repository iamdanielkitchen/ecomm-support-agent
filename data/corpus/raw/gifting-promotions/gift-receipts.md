# Gift receipts and gift returns

If you received a Fieldstone order as a gift, you can start a return even if you do not have the original purchaser's email address. The refund will be issued to the original payment method, and you will receive store credit equal to the item price.

## Starting a return without the purchaser's email

Gift recipients do not need the original buyer's email to begin a return. You will need the order number printed on the packing slip included with your shipment. The order number follows the format FG-NNNNNN.

Visit our returns page and enter the order number along with the shipping address where the gift was delivered. Our system will verify the order and display the eligible items. Select the item you wish to return and follow the prompts to generate a prepaid return label.

If your package did not include a packing slip or the order number is unreadable, a human support specialist can look up the order using your shipping address and an approximate delivery date.

## How refunds work for gift returns

Refunds for gift orders are issued to the original purchaser's payment method. This is a billing system requirement — we cannot redirect refunds to a different card or issue cash equivalents.

As the gift recipient, you will receive a store credit code via email once the warehouse processes your return. The credit matches the full item price and can be used on any future Fieldstone order with no expiration date. The original purchaser will see the refund on their payment method within 5–7 business days of the warehouse receiving your return.

Opened items in original condition are subject to a $7.95 return shipping fee, deducted from the store credit amount. Unopened items return at no cost.

## Gift returns and final-sale items

Items marked as final sale in our catalog cannot be returned, even if received as a gift. Final-sale designation appears on the product page at the time of purchase. Currently, final-sale items include seasonal products such as the Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001) and Seasonal Tea Towels (FG-SAL-002).

If you received a final-sale item as a gift and believe it arrived damaged or defective, contact support with photos of the issue. A specialist will review your case and determine next steps.

## FAQ

**Can I exchange a gift for a different item or size?**  
We do not offer direct exchanges. Return the original item for store credit, then place a new order for the item you prefer. See our guide on how to start a return for step-by-step instructions.

**What if I want to return part of a gift set?**  
Multi-item sets like the Ceramic Mixing Bowl Set (FG-KIT-003) must be returned complete. You cannot return individual pieces from a set and receive partial credit.

**Will the gift giver know I returned their gift?**  
The original purchaser will see the refund transaction on their payment method but will not receive details about which items were returned or why. If you used a gift message or special packaging, visit our guide on gift messages and packaging to learn more about how those features work.
