# Holiday returns: extended window

Orders placed during our holiday shopping period receive an extended return window through mid-January, giving you and your gift recipients extra time. Outside this period, our standard 30-day return policy applies from the delivery date.

## When the extended window applies

The extended return window covers orders placed between November 1 and December 24. These orders can be returned through January 15 of the following year, regardless of when they arrive. This gives you flexibility for early holiday shopping and ensures gift recipients have time to decide after the new year.

If you place an order outside this date range—before November 1 or after December 24—the standard 30-day return window from delivery applies. For details on how the 30-day period works year-round, see our guide on return eligibility and window.

## What the extended window covers

All standard return policies still apply during the holiday period. Items must be unopened or in original condition. Final-sale items marked in the catalog, such as the Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001) and Seasonal Tea Towels (FG-SAL-002), cannot be returned even during the extended window.

Return shipping is free for unopened items. Opened items in original condition have a $7.95 return shipping fee. Refunds land on the original payment method within 5–7 business days of the warehouse receiving your return.

## Starting a holiday return

To start a return, have your order number (format FG-NNNNNN) and the email address used at checkout. The agent Ember will verify your order and generate a prepaid return label if your items are eligible.

Gift recipients can also initiate returns using the order number and email from the original purchase. For more on how gift returns work, including refund destination options, see our article on gift receipts and gift returns.

## FAQ

**When does the holiday return window end each year?**  
Returns for orders placed November 1 through December 24 are accepted through January 15. Orders placed outside that window follow the standard 30-day policy from delivery.

**Can I return a final-sale item if I bought it during the holidays?**  
No. Final-sale items cannot be returned regardless of purchase date. This includes seasonal products like the Holiday Candle and Seasonal Tea Towels.

**I received a gift in late December. Do I have until January 15 to return it?**  
Yes, as long as the order was placed between November 1 and December 24. The extended window applies to the purchase date, not the delivery date.
