# Corporate gifting and wholesale inquiries

Fieldstone Goods welcomes corporate gifting and bulk orders for events, employee appreciation, or client gifts. Orders of 50 units or more require coordination with a human specialist who can discuss custom messaging, volume pricing, and delivery timelines. For smaller bulk purchases (under 50 units), you can place a standard order through the website.

## Minimum order quantities

There is no minimum order quantity for regular purchases. You can buy one item or twenty through the standard checkout process.

For corporate gifting programs that involve 50 or more units, custom packaging, or personalized messages at scale, a specialist will work with you directly. These orders cannot be placed through the self-service checkout. The specialist will confirm product availability, coordinate delivery windows, and provide a detailed quote.

Orders between 10 and 49 units can use standard checkout. If you need a custom message included with each item, mention that in the order notes field. Our fulfillment team will do their best to accommodate the request, though we cannot guarantee personalized notes for every unit in that range.

## Custom messaging and packaging

Standard orders ship with a Fieldstone Goods packing slip and our signature kraft paper wrap. Custom messages are limited to a single note card per order.

For corporate programs requiring unique messaging for each recipient—such as personalized thank-you notes or individual greeting cards—connect with a specialist. They coordinate these details for orders of 50 units or more. Custom packaging options, branded inserts, or co-branded gift boxes are also available at that volume.

If your company plans to send gifts to multiple addresses (such as remote employees or a client list), the specialist can arrange split shipments from a single bulk order. This avoids placing dozens of separate transactions.

## How to request a quote

Corporate and wholesale inquiries route to a human team member who handles pricing, logistics, and custom requests. Ember, our support agent, will connect you with the right specialist when you mention a bulk order or corporate gifting program. See our guide on contacting customer support for the fastest way to reach the team.

When you submit your inquiry, include the following details to speed up the quote process:

- Approximate number of units
- Preferred product SKUs (if known) or a description of what you're looking for
- Delivery timeline or event date
- Whether you need custom messaging, packaging, or split shipments
- Shipping address or addresses

The specialist will typically respond within one business day with a preliminary quote and next steps.

## FAQ

**Can I use a promo code on a corporate order?**  
Promo codes apply to standard checkout orders only. Corporate orders of 50+ units receive volume pricing instead, which the specialist will outline in your quote.

**What if I need the order delivered by a specific date?**  
Mention your deadline when you request a quote. The specialist will confirm whether the timeline is feasible based on current inventory and production capacity. For large orders, we recommend reaching out at least three weeks before your event.

**Are final-sale items available for corporate gifting?**  
Yes. Final-sale products like the Holiday Candle (FG-SAL-001) and Seasonal Tea Towels (FG-SAL-002) can be included in bulk orders. Keep in mind that final-sale items cannot be returned, even in a corporate order.
