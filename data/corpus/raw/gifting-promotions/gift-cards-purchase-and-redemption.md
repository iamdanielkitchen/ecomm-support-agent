# Gift cards: purchase and redemption

Fieldstone Goods offers both digital and physical gift cards in denominations from $25 to $500. Digital cards arrive by email within minutes, while physical cards ship in 3–5 business days. Gift cards never expire and can be combined with most promo codes at checkout.

## Purchasing a gift card

Gift cards are available on our website in $25, $50, $100, $250, and $500 values. Choose digital delivery for instant gifting or select a physical card mailed to your recipient.

When you purchase a digital gift card, the recipient receives an email with a unique redemption code within 15 minutes. You can schedule delivery for a future date if you prefer. Physical gift cards ship to the address you provide using our standard shipping method and arrive in 3–5 business days. Both formats include a personalized message field at checkout.

Gift card purchases are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. They also cannot be used to buy additional gift cards.

## Redeeming a gift card

Enter your gift card code at checkout in the payment section. The code is a 16-character alphanumeric string that appears on the physical card or in the digital delivery email. The balance applies to your order total, including shipping.

If your order exceeds the gift card balance, you can pay the difference with a credit card. If the order total is less than the card value, the remaining balance stays on the gift card for future use. You can check your balance by entering the code on our gift card balance page.

Gift cards apply before promo codes. If you have both, the system deducts the gift card balance first, then applies any active discount to the remaining total. See our guide on promo codes and discounts for details on combining offers.

## Timing and delivery details

Digital gift cards process immediately after purchase and typically arrive within 15 minutes. Check your spam folder if the email does not appear in the recipient's inbox. Physical cards follow the same 3–5 business day timeline as standard orders.

Gift cards have no expiration date and no fees. If a recipient needs to return a gift purchased with a card, the refund goes back to the original gift card code, not a payment method. For more on returns involving gifts, see our article on gift receipts and gift returns.

## FAQ

**Can I use multiple gift cards on one order?**  
Yes. Enter each code separately at checkout. The system will apply all valid balances to your order total.

**What happens if I lose a physical gift card?**  
Contact our support team with your original purchase confirmation. A specialist can help reissue the balance, though this process may take 3–5 business days to verify.

**Do gift cards work on final-sale items?**  
Yes. Gift cards apply to any product in our catalog, including items marked final sale.
