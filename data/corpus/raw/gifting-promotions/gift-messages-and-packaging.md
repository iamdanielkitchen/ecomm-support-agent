# Gift messages and packaging

You can add a handwritten gift message at checkout, and we'll include it in the package without showing any prices. All orders use our standard kraft packaging with a cotton ribbon — we do not offer custom gift wrap or boxes at this time.

## Adding a gift message at checkout

On the shipping page, look for the "Is this a gift?" checkbox. When you check it, a text field appears where you can type your message. Keep your message under 200 characters so it fits on our gift card stock. We print your message on heavyweight paper and tuck it into the package before sealing.

The gift message is optional. If you check "Is this a gift?" without writing anything, we'll still leave the invoice out of the box — you just won't have a card inside.

## What gift packaging looks like

Every order ships in our standard kraft box with a cotton ribbon tied around the outside. The box is sturdy, unbranded, and arrives ready to hand off. We do not add decorative paper, bows, tissue, or custom outer wrap.

Inside, items are wrapped in unbleached tissue and packed with recycled fill. If you added a gift message, the printed card sits on top of the tissue.

## No invoice in the package

When you mark an order as a gift, we never include a packing slip or invoice with pricing. The recipient sees only the products, the tissue, and your message if you wrote one.

If the recipient needs to return or exchange something, they can use the order number from the shipping confirmation email you received. See our guide on gift receipts and gift returns for details on how that process works.

## FAQ

**Can I send the package directly to someone else?**  
Yes. Enter their address as the shipping address at checkout, and make sure to check "Is this a gift?" so no pricing shows up in the box.

**Can I see a preview of the gift card before placing the order?**  
We do not show a preview. Your message is printed exactly as you type it, so double-check spelling and punctuation before you confirm.

**Do you offer gift cards instead of physical products?**  
Yes. We sell digital gift cards in any amount from $25 to $500. The recipient gets an email with a code they can apply at checkout. See our article on gift cards: purchase and redemption for more.
