# Updating the email on an order

The email address you enter at checkout is locked to your order and cannot be changed by our support team. Fieldstone uses your email and order number to verify your identity, so changing it would break that security model. If you mistyped your email, a specialist can help you regain access to your order.

## Why the email cannot be changed

Fieldstone does not use user accounts. Instead, every order is tied to the email address you provided at checkout. When you look up an order or request a return, we send a verification link to that email to confirm it's really you. Changing the email after the fact would allow someone else to hijack your order with only the order number, which is visible on shipping labels and receipts.

Because of this security design, the email is permanent once your order is placed. See our guide on How Fieldstone order numbers work for more about how we verify your identity.

## What to do if you mistyped your email

If you entered the wrong email address and cannot receive order updates or return instructions, contact our support team. A specialist will verify your identity using your billing information and the order details, then manually assist with returns, refunds, or shipping questions.

You will not be able to use the self-service order lookup tool, but the specialist can handle everything on your behalf. This process takes one business day for most requests.

## What support can help with

Even though the email on your order cannot be updated, our team can still:

- Process a return or refund manually after verifying your identity
- Resend shipping confirmations to a corrected email address (the original order email stays unchanged in our system)
- Update the shipping address if your order has not shipped yet
- Answer questions about your order status or delivery timeline

For urgent issues like a pending delivery to the wrong address, contact support as soon as possible. Our article on contacting customer support includes response times and what information to have ready.

## FAQ

**Can I update my email for future orders?**  
Yes. Each order is independent, so you can use a different email address the next time you check out. There is no account to update because Fieldstone does not store customer profiles between orders.

**What if I used a work email and no longer have access to it?**  
Contact support with your order number, billing address, and the last four digits of the payment method. A specialist will verify your identity and help you complete your return or answer your question without needing access to the original email.

**Will my order confirmation still go to the wrong email?**  
Yes. The confirmation, shipping updates, and any automated messages will continue to go to the email you entered at checkout. If you need those details, a support specialist can forward them to your corrected address after verifying your identity.
