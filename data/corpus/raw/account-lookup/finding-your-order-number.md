# Finding your order number

Your Fieldstone order number is the six-digit code we send after every purchase, formatted FG-NNNNNN. You need this number to track shipments, start a return, or reach our support team. It appears in three emails we send automatically — check your inbox and spam folder for messages from orders@fieldstonegoods.com.

## Where your order number appears

**Confirmation email subject line**  
Within minutes of checkout, you receive a confirmation email with a subject like "Your Fieldstone order FG-123456 is confirmed." The number sits right in the subject, making it easy to search.

**Confirmation email body**  
Open that same email and scroll to the top. Just below the "Thank you" heading, you'll see your order number in bold, followed by your order date and total. This is the clearest place to copy the full number.

**Shipping notification email**  
When your order ships, we send a second email with the subject "Your Fieldstone order has shipped." The body includes your order number, tracking link, and estimated delivery window. If you saved the shipping email but deleted the confirmation, this is a reliable backup.

## If you cannot locate the email

Start by searching your inbox for "fieldstonegoods.com" or "FG-" to surface any message containing the number. Check your spam and promotions folders — confirmation emails sometimes land there.

If the email is truly missing, contact our support team at help@fieldstonegoods.com. Include the email address you used at checkout and an approximate order date. A specialist will locate your order and reply with your number, usually within one business day. See our guide on How Fieldstone order numbers work for details on the format and what each part means.

## Why you need your order number

Fieldstone does not use customer accounts in the traditional sense. Instead, we verify your identity with your email address and order number each time you interact with us. When you request a return, check order status, or ask a question, you'll enter both pieces of information. Keep your confirmation email in a safe folder so you can reference it quickly.

## FAQ

**Can I use my shipping tracking number instead of my order number?**  
No. The tracking number identifies the carrier's shipment, but our systems require the Fieldstone order number (FG-NNNNNN) to pull up your purchase details, process returns, or apply adjustments.

**What if I placed multiple orders and lost track of which number belongs to which purchase?**  
Each confirmation email lists the items in that specific order. Search your inbox for the product name or SKU to narrow down the right message. If you're still unsure, email help@fieldstonegoods.com with the items you're asking about and we'll match them to the correct order.

**I found an old order number — does it expire?**  
Order numbers remain valid indefinitely. Even for purchases outside the 30-day return window, the number still works for order history inquiries and warranty questions.
