# How Fieldstone order numbers work

Every Fieldstone order gets a unique identifier in the format FG-NNNNNN, where the six digits make your order trackable. This number is the primary way our support team verifies your identity and pulls up your order details. You'll need it for returns, tracking questions, and any other help with your purchase.

Because Fieldstone doesn't use traditional user accounts, your order number combined with the email address used at checkout is how you confirm your identity. Think of the order number as your receipt — keep it handy, especially if you're reaching out for support.

## What the format means

Fieldstone order numbers always start with "FG-" followed by six digits. For example: FG-123456. This format makes it easy to distinguish order numbers from other reference codes like tracking numbers or SKUs. You'll see it prominently in your order confirmation email, shipping notifications, and packing slip.

The six-digit sequence is unique to your order. No two orders share the same number, so providing this identifier ensures support is looking at the right purchase.

## Where to find your order number

Your order number appears in the subject line and body of your order confirmation email, which is sent immediately after checkout. It's also printed at the top of your packing slip inside the shipping box. See our guide on finding your order number for screenshots and additional locations.

If you've deleted your confirmation email, check your shipping notification — the order number appears there as well. The tracking page linked in that email also displays the order number once you arrive.

## Why support needs this number

When you contact Fieldstone with a question about an order, the support agent (or our assistant Ember) will ask for your order number and the email address used at checkout. This two-part verification lets us locate your order and confirm it's yours before discussing details or processing a return.

Without the order number, we cannot retrieve your order information. Fieldstone doesn't store purchase history under an account dashboard, so the order number is the only direct path to your order record.

## FAQ

**What if I have multiple orders?** Each order has its own unique FG-NNNNNN number, even if placed on the same day with the same email address. You'll receive separate confirmation emails for each order, and each will include its distinct order number.

**Can I look up my order with just my email address?** No. For security and accuracy, we require both the order number and the email address. The order number ensures we're reviewing the specific purchase you're asking about, especially if you've placed multiple orders.

**Is the order number the same as a tracking number?** No. Your tracking number comes from the shipping carrier (like USPS or FedEx) and appears in your shipping notification. The order number is Fieldstone's internal identifier. Both are useful, but support requests should always include the FG-NNNNNN order number.
