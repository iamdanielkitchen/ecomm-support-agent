# Order history without an account

Fieldstone does not use traditional customer accounts. Instead, you access your orders using your email address and order number. Every confirmation email contains the information you need to look up your order status, track shipments, and start returns.

This guide explains how to retrieve past orders, what information you'll need, and how the system keeps your orders organized.

## How to find a specific order

Each order confirmation email includes your unique order number in the format FG-NNNNNN (for example, FG-123456). To look up an order:

1. Open the confirmation email for that order
2. Note the order number in the subject line or email body
3. Visit the order lookup page and enter your email address and order number
4. View your order details, tracking information, and available actions

If you cannot locate the confirmation email, check your spam folder or search your inbox for "Fieldstone order confirmation." See our guide on Finding your order number for additional tips.

## Accessing multiple orders

Because Fieldstone does not maintain a dashboard of all your purchases, you'll need to reference each confirmation email individually. If you've placed several orders:

- Keep confirmation emails in a dedicated folder
- Search your inbox by order number (FG-) to see all Fieldstone purchases
- Use the same email address for future orders to keep everything in one inbox

Each order stands alone. You cannot view a combined history, but your confirmation emails serve as a complete record of everything you've ordered.

## What you can do with order lookup

Once you enter your email and order number, you can:

- Check the current shipping status and carrier tracking link
- Review which items are in the order and their prices
- Start a return for eligible items within the 30-day window
- Confirm your delivery address

You do not need to create an account to perform any of these actions. The lookup system handles returns, exchanges, and order updates.

## Account features in development

Fieldstone is building optional account features for customers who want a centralized view of their order history. These features are not yet available, but when they launch, you'll be able to:

- See all past orders in one place
- Save shipping addresses for faster checkout
- Opt in to reorder reminders for frequently purchased items

For now, your confirmation emails remain the primary way to track and manage orders. They contain everything you need to get help, check status, or initiate a return.

## FAQ

**What if I used different email addresses for different orders?**  
Each order is tied to the email address you provided at checkout. You'll need to look up orders separately using the matching email and order number for each one.

**Can I update my email address after placing an order?**  
Email addresses cannot be changed after an order is placed, since the confirmation email serves as your access credential. Future orders can use a different address if needed.

**Do I need to keep the confirmation email forever?**  
Keep confirmation emails at least until your return window closes (30 days from delivery). After that, they're useful for warranty reference or reordering the same items.
