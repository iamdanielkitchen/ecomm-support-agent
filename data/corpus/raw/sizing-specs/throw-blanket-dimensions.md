# Linen throw blanket dimensions

The Linen Throw Blanket (FG-HOM-001) measures 50 inches by 70 inches. This is a standard throw size that works beautifully draped over furniture or as light coverage at the foot of a bed. The woven linen softens with each wash, so the blanket becomes more supple and inviting over time.

## Size and coverage

At 50 by 70 inches, the throw fits comfortably across a sofa or draped over an armchair. It covers about two-thirds of a queen or king bed when folded at the foot, offering an extra layer without overwhelming the bedding. The proportions suit one or two people for couch reading or watching a movie.

If you want full-body coverage, drape the throw lengthwise. At 70 inches, it extends from shoulders to feet for most adults. The 50-inch width wraps around one person or covers two people sitting close together. The blanket is not intended as a primary bedding layer—it functions as accent coverage or seasonal warmth.

## How linen behaves

Linen feels slightly textured and crisp when new. After the first wash, the fibers begin to relax. By the third or fourth wash cycle, the blanket develops a soft drape that conforms to your body or the furniture beneath it. This is normal and desirable. See our guide on linen washing instructions for care details that preserve the fabric's character.

The throw has a 1-inch hemmed edge on all sides. Linen can wrinkle easily, which is part of its appeal—the lived-in look suits casual spaces. If you prefer crisp edges, a warm iron on the linen setting smooths the fabric. Most customers leave it as-is.

## Comparing to other textiles

Cotton throws in this size range feel heavier and hold their shape more rigidly. Wool throws offer more insulation but less breathability. Linen balances weight and airflow, so it works across three seasons without feeling too warm or too light. The throw weighs about 1.8 pounds, light enough to toss in a tote bag for outdoor events.

For more on how we source and finish our linen, see about our linen. All Fieldstone linen products use European flax and are pre-washed once before shipment, so you will notice some softness immediately.

## FAQ

**Does the throw shrink after washing?** Expect up to 2% shrinkage in length and width after the first wash, so the dimensions may shift to approximately 49 by 68.5 inches. This is typical for linen and accounted for in the original cut.

**Can I use this as a picnic blanket?** Yes, though linen is more delicate than canvas or synthetic outdoor blankets. The throw works well on grass or sand for a few hours. Shake out debris and wash afterward to prevent staining.

**Will the throw pill or fray?** High-quality linen resists pilling. The hemmed edges are double-stitched to prevent fraying, but rough surfaces or pet claws can snag the weave. Trim any loose threads with scissors rather than pulling them.
