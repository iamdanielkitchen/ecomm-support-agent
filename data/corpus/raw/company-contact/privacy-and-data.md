# Privacy and data practices

We collect only what we need to fulfill your order and provide support. You have the right to request a copy of your data or ask us to delete it at any time. This article explains what information we collect, how long we keep it, and how to submit a privacy request.

## What information we collect

When you place an order, we collect your email address, shipping address, and payment details. Payment information goes directly to our payment processor and is not stored on our servers. We keep your email and order number (format FG-NNNNNN) to verify your identity when you track a shipment or request a return.

We also collect the browsing activity necessary to complete your transaction, such as the items you added to your cart and the shipping method you selected. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or sell your information to third parties.

## How long we keep your data

Order records, including your email and shipping details, are retained for seven years to comply with tax and accounting requirements. After that period, records are automatically deleted unless you have an open return or dispute.

If you contact support, we keep a record of your inquiry and our response for two years. This helps us provide consistent service if you reach out again about the same order. See our guide on contacting customer support for more about how we handle your questions.

## Requesting a copy or deletion of your data

You can request a copy of all the personal information we have on file, or ask us to delete it entirely. To submit a privacy request, email your order number and a brief description of what you need to the support address listed in the contacting customer support article. A specialist will verify your identity using the email address on the order and respond within 30 days.

Deletion requests remove your email, shipping address, and support history from our active systems. Order numbers and transaction amounts may be retained in anonymized financial records to meet legal obligations, but they will no longer be linked to your name or contact information.

If you have an open return or refund in progress, we cannot complete a deletion request until that matter is resolved. Once the return window closes and any refund has been processed, deletion can proceed.

## FAQ

**Can I opt out of order confirmation emails?**  
No. Confirmation and shipping emails are transactional and required to complete your purchase. You will not receive marketing emails unless you separately subscribe to our newsletter, which has its own opt-out link in every message.

**Does Fieldstone share data with partners?**  
We share the minimum necessary information with our shipping carrier and payment processor to fulfill your order. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

**What happens to my data if I never complete checkout?**  
Cart contents are stored temporarily in your browser session and are not linked to any personal information unless you proceed to enter an email address at checkout. Abandoned carts are cleared after 24 hours.
