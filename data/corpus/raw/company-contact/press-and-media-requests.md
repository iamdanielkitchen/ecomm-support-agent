# Press and media requests

Fieldstone Goods welcomes editorial coverage, product reviews, and brand collaboration opportunities. Media inquiries are handled by a dedicated team that coordinates interviews, provides product samples, and licenses images for publication. This article explains how to submit a request and what to expect.

## Submitting a media inquiry

For all press, editorial, and partnership inquiries, email **press@fieldstonegoodsinc.com** with the following information:

- Your name, publication or platform, and role
- The nature of your request (interview, product review, image licensing, or collaboration)
- Intended publication date or deadline
- Any specific products or topics you want to cover

A member of the media relations team will respond within two business days. If your deadline is urgent, note that in the subject line.

## Product samples for editorial review

Writers and content creators working on editorial features may request product samples. Include your media kit or portfolio link, the outlet's audience size or reach, and which specific products you would like to review. Only items from the current catalog are available for sampling.

The team evaluates requests based on editorial focus and alignment with Fieldstone's brand. Approved reviewers receive samples at no charge, with no obligation to publish a positive review. You will receive tracking information once the sample ships, typically within five business days of approval.

## Image licensing and brand assets

High-resolution product images, lifestyle photography, and the Fieldstone logo are available for editorial use with prior approval. Requests must include the publication name, story context, and intended usage (print, web, or social media). Images cannot be altered beyond standard cropping and color correction.

Commercial use, including advertising or paid partnerships, requires a separate licensing agreement. Contact the press team to discuss terms.

## Interviews and brand storytelling

Journalists writing feature stories or profiles may request interviews with Fieldstone leadership or product specialists. The media team coordinates scheduling and provides background materials. Interview requests should include your story angle, outlet, and preferred timeline. For general background on our company and mission, see our guide on About Fieldstone Goods.

If your inquiry falls outside media relations—such as a customer service question or order issue—visit Contacting customer support to reach the right team.

## FAQ

**Can I use Fieldstone product images in a blog post without permission?**  
Editorial bloggers with a clear review or feature context may request image licensing at no charge. Submit a brief description of your post and reach to press@fieldstonegoodsinc.com.

**How long does sample approval take?**  
Most sample requests receive a response within two business days. Approved samples ship within five business days, arriving via standard shipping (3–5 business days).

**Do you sponsor content creators or influencers?**  
Paid partnerships and influencer sponsorships are managed separately from editorial media relations. Include "partnership inquiry" in your email subject line, and the team will direct you to the appropriate contact.
