# Contacting customer support

You can reach Fieldstone support through live chat or email. Chat with Ember, our support agent, for instant help with order tracking, returns, and product questions. For issues that need a specialist—like billing disputes or complex delivery problems—Ember will connect you with the right team.

## How to contact us

**Live chat** is the fastest way to get help. Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner of any page on fieldstonegoods.com. Ember is available 24/7 and can look up orders, start returns, answer product questions, and help with most order issues on the spot.

**Email** is available if you prefer it or if chat is unavailable. Send your question to support@fieldstonegoods.com. Include your order number (format FG-NNNNNN) and the email address you used at checkout. Response time is typically within 12 hours during business days.

## Information that speeds things up

Having these details ready helps us resolve your question faster:

- **Order number** — Starts with FG- and appears in your confirmation email. Example: FG-123456.
- **Email address** — The one you used at checkout. We use this to verify your identity.
- **Photos** — If your issue involves damaged items or packaging problems, attach clear photos showing the issue.
- **Tracking number** — For delivery questions, include the tracking number from your shipment confirmation email.

If you are asking about a return, see our guide on [Delivery issues and lost packages](#) for tracking help, or [Duplicate orders and billing questions](#) if you were charged incorrectly.

## Response windows

**Urgent issues** like missing packages or incorrect items typically get a reply within one hour during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM ET). Ember handles most urgent requests immediately in chat.

**Standard questions** about returns, product details, or order changes usually receive a response within 12 hours. If a human specialist needs to review your case—such as for billing disputes or corporate order inquiries—expect a reply within one business day.

**Complex cases** that require warehouse investigation or escalation may take 2–3 business days. We will keep you updated if your issue needs additional time.

## FAQ

**Can I call Fieldstone support?** We do not currently offer phone support. Chat and email let us research your order thoroughly and provide accurate answers without wait times.

**What if I need help outside business hours?** Ember is available in chat 24/7 and can handle most order questions immediately. Email responses may be delayed until the next business day if sent on weekends or holidays.

**Do I need to create an account to contact support?** No. We verify your identity using your order number and email address, so you can get help without logging in.
