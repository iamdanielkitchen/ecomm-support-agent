# About Fieldstone Goods

Fieldstone Goods started in 2019 as a curation project: founder Mara Chen spent six months visiting workshops in Vermont, Pennsylvania, and the Pacific Northwest, asking makers what they would keep in their own kitchens and homes. The first catalog had eleven items. Today we carry a curated selection of kitchen essentials, home textiles, and seasonal goods—each sourced from small producers and built to last years, not seasons.

We use the term "slow-build" to describe how our catalog grows. Instead of monthly drops or trend cycles, we add products only when we find a maker whose work meets our standards for material honesty and functional design. Some items stay in the catalog for years. Others rotate based on seasonal availability or small-batch production limits.

## What Fieldstone does

We work directly with domestic workshops and small manufacturers. Every product ships from our warehouse in Asheville, North Carolina. Orders are packed by a team of twelve, most of whom have been with us since the beginning.

Our catalog focuses on kitchen tools, natural-fiber textiles, and home goods that serve a clear purpose. We do not carry apparel, electronics, or décor items that exist primarily for visual novelty. When we mark an item final sale, it is usually because the maker produced a limited seasonal run or because we are discontinuing that SKU to make room for something new.

We handle our own order fulfillment, customer support, and returns processing. See our guide on sustainability and packaging for details on how we ship and what materials we use.

## What Fieldstone does not do

We do not operate fulfillment centers outside the United States. We do not white-label products or source from overseas factories that do not allow site visits. We do not offer same-day delivery, subscription boxes, or influencer collaborations.

We do not have a loyalty program or user accounts in the current version of our shop. You identify yourself using the email address and order number from your confirmation.

Fieldstone does not wholesale to large chains. We do work with independent retailers, design studios, and hospitality clients on trade terms. For those inquiries, see our article on wholesale and trade inquiries.

## FAQ

**Who owns Fieldstone Goods?**  
Fieldstone is privately owned by Mara Chen and operates as an LLC. We have no outside investors and no parent company.

**Do you have a physical store?**  
We do not have a retail storefront. All orders ship from our Asheville warehouse. Occasionally we participate in regional craft markets; those dates appear on our homepage when scheduled.

**Can I suggest a product or maker?**  
We welcome maker introductions. Email the details through the contact form on our homepage. We review suggestions quarterly but cannot respond to every submission.
