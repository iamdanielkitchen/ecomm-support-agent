# Accessibility statement

Fieldstone Goods is committed to making our website usable for all customers. We aim to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and continually test with assistive technologies including screen readers, keyboard navigation, and voice control software. If you encounter a barrier while browsing or placing an order, we want to know—and we will help you complete your purchase through an alternative method.

## Our accessibility standards

We design our site to work with common assistive technologies. This includes:

- **Keyboard navigation**: All interactive elements can be accessed without a mouse.
- **Screen reader support**: Product descriptions, checkout fields, and order confirmations are labeled for clarity with JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver.
- **Color contrast**: Text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios against backgrounds.
- **Scalable text**: Pages remain functional when text is resized up to 200% in browser settings.
- **Alternative text**: Product images include descriptive alt text.

We test across browsers and devices, but we know barriers can still appear. When they do, we treat them as bugs and prioritize fixes.

## Reporting an accessibility issue

If you find a page, feature, or checkout step that does not work with your assistive technology, please let us know. Contact our support team with:

- A description of the barrier (for example, "the promo code field is not announced by my screen reader")
- The page or URL where you encountered it
- The assistive technology and browser you are using

See our guide on **Contacting customer support** for email and chat options. A specialist will respond within one business day. We will work with you to resolve the issue and, if needed, complete your order over the phone or via email.

## Alternative ordering methods

You can place an order without using the website:

- **Email**: Send your shipping address and the SKUs or product names you want to order. We will reply with a total and payment link.
- **Phone assistance**: A support agent can read product descriptions, check availability, and process payment over the phone during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9 a.m.–6 p.m. EST).

Returns, exchanges, and order tracking are also available through these channels. You will never need to navigate the site to resolve an issue if it is not accessible to you.

## Ongoing improvements

We review our site quarterly with accessibility consultants and incorporate user feedback into each update. Our current focus areas include improving mobile touch target sizing and adding more detailed product dimension information for assistive technology users.

If you have suggestions beyond bug reports—features that would make shopping easier or content that should be structured differently—we welcome that input as well.
