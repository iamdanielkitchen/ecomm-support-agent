# Wholesale and trade inquiries

Fieldstone Goods partners with independent retailers and design trade professionals who share our commitment to quality home goods. If you're interested in carrying our products in your store or specifying them for client projects, we welcome wholesale and trade inquiries. This article explains who we work with, what to expect, and how to request a line sheet.

## Who we partner with

We work with two primary groups: independent retail stores and interior design trade professionals. Independent retailers include specialty home goods shops, lifestyle boutiques, and gift stores that curate collections for local customers. Design trade partners include interior designers, architects, and decorators who specify furnishings and home accessories for residential or commercial projects.

We do not currently work with online-only resellers or drop-ship arrangements. Our wholesale program is built for partners who maintain a physical location or design practice and can present Fieldstone products in a thoughtful context.

## Minimum orders and pricing

Wholesale orders have a $500 minimum at wholesale pricing. Opening orders for new accounts typically start at $1,000 to establish a working relationship and ensure a meaningful selection of products. Reorders follow the $500 minimum.

Wholesale pricing is typically 50% off retail for most items. Line sheets include wholesale cost, suggested retail price, and case pack quantities where applicable. Trade pricing for design professionals varies by project scope and is discussed during the inquiry process.

Payment terms for established accounts are net 30. New accounts pay via credit card or bank transfer at the time of order until credit terms are approved.

## How to request a line sheet

To request a wholesale line sheet or discuss a trade project, contact our wholesale team directly. Because Ember, our support agent, handles individual customer orders and returns, wholesale inquiries go to a human specialist who can discuss pricing, minimums, and partnership details.

Include your business name, location, and a brief description of your retail store or design practice in your inquiry. If you have a website or portfolio, share that as well. A specialist will respond within two business days with a line sheet and next steps.

For corporate gifting or bulk orders that do not require ongoing wholesale terms, see our guide on corporate gifting and wholesale for information on one-time large orders.

## FAQ

**Do you offer samples for prospective wholesale accounts?**  
We can arrange sample orders at a discount for serious retail or trade inquiries. The specialist reviewing your request will discuss sample options and any associated costs during your initial conversation.

**Can I carry Fieldstone products if I also sell online?**  
Independent retailers with both physical and online storefronts are welcome to apply. We ask that online resale align with our brand presentation standards, which the wholesale team will review with approved accounts.

**How long does wholesale account approval take?**  
Most inquiries receive a response within two business days. If your business is a good fit, you'll receive a line sheet and account application. Once the application is submitted, approval typically takes three to five business days.
